International School of Commerce - Glen Rock NJ School for Insurance Agents

The International School of Commerce was founded in 1981 and has been offering Insurance Licensing, Continuing Education and Insurance Institute Courses for NJ state insurance licensing.. Presently an on site insurance school operating out of the offices of D & G Sayles Corp. in Glen Rock NJ, the insurance school Director is Jean Huber, AAI, AIC, ARM, CPIW. For scheduling of classes on or off-premises, please contact Jean Huber at jean_huber@dsayles.com. If you have any questions regarding courses or possible topics of CE Classes, please do not hesitate to contact Jean Huber at (201) 652-0407, ext. 24.

If you wish to register for a continuing education class, please visit the 'About Us' page and fax, call or email us. You can also click on the Course Registration link and send us a completed form right now.
