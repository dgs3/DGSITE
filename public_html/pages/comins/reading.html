Further Reading

The following books have additional information regarding International Marine Insurance.

  • Winter, William D.
    Marine Insurance: Its Principles and Practice [1919].
    - The 'bible' of marine insurance. An authoritative text that reads easily. Its only shortcoming is its publication date. But, marine insurance being what it is, there have been very few changes since 1957.
  • Bickelhaupt, David L.
    General Insurance (The Iriwn Series in Insurance and Economic Security).
    - A Typical Richard D. Irwin text; authoritative but very, very dry. You'll likely find the information you want in fewer pages than Winter's text, at the expense of being bored to death.
  • Hinkelman, Edward.
    Dictionary of International Trade: 4,071 International Trade, Economic, Banking, Legal & Shipping Terms.
  • Nelson, Carl A.
    Import/Export: How to Get Started in International Trade.
    - A well-regarded primer on Marine Insurance; how to import and export from a to z in a clear, concise language.
  • U.S. Customs Service.
    A Basic Guide to Importing.
