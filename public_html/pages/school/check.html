NJ Dept. of Banking Continued Ed Credits

Please read the directions throughly before going to the link below:

  1. Go To http://www.ce.psiexams.com
  2. Click on the 'Lookup My continuing Education Transcript' link.
  3. Under state, select 'New Jersey' (or applicable state), then fill in your License Number and Last Name.

Remember the transcript includes all CE classes posted back to the beginning without any total, so you should consider your license renewal dates.
