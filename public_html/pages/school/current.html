Property/Casualty Licensing Courses

The International School of Commerce offers the New Jersey Property/Casualty Licensing Course (40 Hours) in a day class during the hours of 8 AM to 5 PM, on an ongoing basis. Please call for more details 201.652.0407.

The International School of Commerce also offers the 40 hour New Jersey Property/Casualty (including Health) Prelicensing Course at our Glen Rock location. Please call 201.652.0407 for the current schedule.

The cost of either course is $300. This includes the cost of books and course outline.

Insurance Institute Courses

International School of Commerce occasionally offers AAI 81 (Part 1 of 3) in the Accredited Advisor in Insurance Designation. Please call 201.652.0407 for the current schedule. Participants in this course earn 8 CE Credits for attending class and another 12 if they pass the Insurance Institute National Exam. This course deals with an introduction to insurance, sales, legal concepts, risk management, account development, personal lines, and commercial property coverages. Call Jean for details 201.652.0407, ext. 24).

The cost of the course is $350. This does not include book costs.
